My projects

I am a bioinformatician at the ICO (Institut de Cancérologie de l'Ouest). I am currently working on the processing of exomic data of metastatic breast cancer RH+/HER2-. I previously did a PhD in bacterial genomics with the aim to allow the rapid identification of bacterial strains using the Oxford Nanopore MinION sequencer and focusing on the Streptococcus thermophilus species. My professional project is to work in fields such as genomics, transcriptomics and/or metagenomics.
